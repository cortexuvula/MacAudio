Installation Complete

MacAudio has been installed successfully.

Getting started:

  1. Open MacAudio from your Applications folder.
  2. Grant Microphone access when prompted.
  3. Grant Screen & System Audio Recording access in System Settings > Privacy & Security.
  4. Click Start in the menu bar icon.
  5. Select “MacAudio” as the input device in your audio/video app.
Tip: MacAudio runs in the menu bar. Look for its icon at the top of your screen.
Note: Audio playback was briefly interrupted during installation because the audio system was restarted to load the new driver.

Troubleshooting

If the “MacAudio” device does not appear in your audio apps:

  1. Restart your Mac.
  2. Verify the driver is loaded: open Terminal and run system_profiler SPAudioDataType | grep MacAudio

Uninstalling

To remove MacAudio, run the Uninstall MacAudio script included in the DMG, or manually:

  1. Quit MacAudio from the menu bar.
  2. Delete /Applications/MacAudio.app
  3. Delete /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/HAL/MacAudioDriver.driver (requires admin)
  4. Restart your Mac or run sudo killall coreaudiod
