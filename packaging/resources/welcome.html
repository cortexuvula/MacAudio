MacAudio Installer

MacAudio captures your microphone and system audio, mixes them together, and makes the mix available as a virtual audio input device.

This installer will place:

  • MacAudio.app in /Applications
  • MacAudioDriver.driver in /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/HAL

An administrator password is required because the audio driver must be installed to a system directory.

Requires macOS 14.2 or later.
